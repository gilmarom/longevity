Our Why ?

Shaping now a future world in which human beings will choose and create the life they want at any age With vitality, is our commitment.

Today, aging is not defined as a disease by the World Health Organization, but scientists are already see a connection between diseases like Parkinson's and Alzheimer's as part of the aging process. In order to accelerate existing research, to establish an industry and to dare to try new directions that sometimes do not fit with the same considerations of grantees for research, we need to raise awareness about this issue and enable talented people to access the field.
